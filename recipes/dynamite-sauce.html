Dynamite sauce

Dynamite sauce is a spicy, creamy sauce used in or on top of many sushi dishes. It also works as a great dipping sauce. Can be refrigerated for up to one week.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon chili garlic sauce

Recipe instructions

  1. Mix mayonnaise, sugar, and chili garlic sauce together in a small bowl until well combined.
